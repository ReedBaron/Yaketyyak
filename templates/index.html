Ollama installs automatically on first run.

First launch on macOS

macOS blocks apps from unidentified developers by default. Here's how to open Yakety Yak:

  1. Unzip the download — you'll see Yakety Yak.app
  2. Drag it into your /Applications folder
  3. Right-click (or Control-click) the app and choose Open
  4. Click Open in the dialog that appears

You only need to do this once. After that, it opens normally from your dock.
